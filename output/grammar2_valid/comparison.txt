======================================================================
  COMPARISON: SLR(1) vs LR(1)
======================================================================

Metric                          SLR(1)           LR(1)          
--------------------------------------------------------------
Number of states                12               22             
ACTION entries                  36               56             
GOTO entries                    9                15             
Total table entries             45               71             
Build time (ms)                 4                4               ms
Has conflicts                   No               No             
--------------------------------------------------------------

Analysis:
  Both parsers handle this grammar without conflicts.
  SLR(1) uses fewer states (12) versus LR(1) (22).

Conclusion:
  LR(1) is strictly more powerful than SLR(1) because it uses
  per-item lookaheads instead of global FOLLOW sets for reductions.
  This precision avoids spurious conflicts at the cost of a potentially
  larger number of states.
